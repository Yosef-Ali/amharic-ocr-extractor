## Email Template (Formal — for universities, churches)

Subject: Free Amharic OCR Tool — Looking for Early Testers

Dear [Name],

I've developed a web application that uses AI to extract Amharic text from
scanned PDF documents and images. It achieves high accuracy on modern
Amharic print including:

- Catholic and Orthodox prayer books
- Bible pages (both modern Amharic and older editions)
- Novels and short stories
- Formal documents and publications

The tool preserves fidel characters accurately (distinguishing ሀ/ሐ/ኀ,
ሰ/ሠ, ጸ/ፀ and all Ethiopic punctuation) and supports two-column layout
detection.

I'm looking for 3-5 early testers who work with Amharic documents
regularly. Would you be willing to try it for 10 minutes and share your
experience?

Thank you for your time.

## Feedback Questions (Ask after they try it)

1. What document did you upload? (prayer book, novel, Bible, other?)
2. How accurate was the extracted text? (0-10)
3. Did the fidel characters look correct?
4. What would you use this for in your daily work?
5. Would you pay $10/month for this tool? Why or why not?
6. What's the one thing that would make this much more useful?
